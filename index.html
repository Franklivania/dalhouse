Dalhousie University Admissions Application

Applicants to the University of King's College also use this form to apply for admission.



Note: For the Dalhousie Undergraduate and King's Applications for Fall 2025 as well as Medicine, Dentistry and Law, You will be submitting in our new Application Portal. Apply here.


For Graduate Programs, you will apply starting on this page.

Note: the application deadline for the Master of Nursing - Mental Health & Addiction has been extended to December 1, 2024

 How to login:

First Time Student: Please complete the "First time student account creation" form.

Returning Student: To complete an in-progress application or check on the status of a submitted application, please enter your login ID and PIN below. If you have forgotten your PIN, please email the Admissions Office for assistance.

Current or previous Dal/King's Students: Login through Dal Online.
Please Note:
  • Online applications are available until midnight (Atlantic Time) of the deadline date.


    • Applicants must be aware documents can take up to 10 business days to be added to an application.


  • For a list of the deadlines, please see

    Dalhousie University Application Deadlines for Undergraduate Programs and Application Deadlines for Graduate Programs

    • or

    University of King's College Important Dates and Deadlines
